Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your MixTape Premium subscription at any time through the app settings. Upon cancellation:

  • You will retain Premium benefits until the end of your current billing period
  • No further charges will be made after the current period ends
  • Your account will revert to the free tier automatically

2. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds in the following circumstances:

  • Accidental Purchase: Full refund within 48 hours of purchase
  • Technical Issues: If Premium features are unavailable due to service problems
  • Billing Errors: Duplicate charges or incorrect billing amounts

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are not provided for:

  • Subscriptions canceled after 48 hours of purchase
  • Partial billing periods (no prorated refunds)
  • Account violations or suspensions
  • Change of mind after the 48-hour window

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at support@mixtapeapp.com
  2. Include your account email and reason for refund request
  3. Provide transaction details (date, amount, payment method)

Refund requests are typically processed within 5-7 business days.

5. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be:

  • Issued to the original payment method used
  • Processed within 5-7 business days
  • Subject to your bank's processing times (may take additional days to appear)

6. Subscription Changes

If you change your subscription tier:

  • Upgrade: Charged the difference immediately, new benefits apply instantly
  • Downgrade: Change takes effect at the end of current billing period

7. Free Trials

If we offer free trial periods:

  • Cancel before the trial ends to avoid charges
  • Cancellations during trials are immediate with no charges
  • One free trial per user

8. Disputes

If you have a billing dispute, please contact us before initiating a chargeback. We're committed to resolving issues fairly and promptly.

9. Contact for Refunds

For refund requests or billing questions:

Email: support@mixtapeapp.com

Response Time: Within 24-48 hours

10. Policy Updates

We may update this refund policy. Continued use of Premium services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
